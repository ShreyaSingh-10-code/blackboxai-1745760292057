Effective Study Tips

  1. Set clear, achievable goals for each study session.
  2. Use active learning techniques like summarizing and questioning.
  3. Take regular breaks to maintain focus and avoid burnout.
  4. Practice past papers and quizzes to test your understanding.
  5. Stay organized with a study schedule and prioritize tasks.
